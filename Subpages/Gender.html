Sex vs. Gender

People sometimes use "Sex" and "Gender" interchangeably, but they are actually two very different things. Sex is a biological term based on body parts and such while Gender refers to our internal sense and understanding of ourselves.


The Gender Spectrum

Some Terms to Know

  • Ally - Someone who is not a part of the LGBTQ+ community who still chooses to support those who are actually a part of the community.
  • Cisgender - A person who's gender aligns with the sex they were assigned at birth.
  • Gender Expression - How one externally expresses their gender (Like how they dress or how they act, for example).
